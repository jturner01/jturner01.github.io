Harmony: Start making connections based on something more

Music is the world's most popular language
The Purpose
Making meaningful connections with people these days is tough. First impressions are often completely physical, and while there is nothing wrong with that, we want the connection to be based on something more. Something real. Something deeper. Something true.
The Interface
Harmony is a social interface that allows users interested in connecting with others to first see with their ears --- listen to what other users say represents them, check out playlists on their profile, and send suggestions to add to their lives!

We connect deeply with the music we listen to.
Now let that music connect you with others.

Your Soul Song
These 3-5 minutes of audio will be other users' first impression of you, before anything else
Start a Conversation
If you like what you hear, follow the music and start a conversation with another user
Share More
Unlock playlists on your profile to let your connection learn more about you, send songs to them that you think they would like, and create common playlists
